Ramen Noodles

Ramen is a noodle delicacy and comes in a perfectly wrapped package, the package surounds the uncooked noodle and preserves its freshness. Within the package comes with a small asortment of amazing delicacies including the perfect uncooked ramen and a mouth watering flavor package.

Needed Material/Ingredients

  • Ramen Package with Season packet(included)
  • Water
  • Metal Pot
  • stove

Cooking Instructions

  1. Add water to metal pot until its with at minimum 2 cups of water.
  2. Place pot onto the stove and wait until water boils.
  3. Unwrap the Ramen Package and place it delicately into the boiling water.
  4. let the rament sit for at least 3 min in the boiling water then remove the water but leave enough for to mix with the flavor packet.
  5. Tear flavor package and stir well until the seasoning is completely desolved.
  6. Place ramen into a bowl and enjoy!
