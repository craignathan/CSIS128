Karate Kid

A Story of a kid who moves from the Detroit to China and begins to get bullied for being from the US. He get taken in as a pupil to a martial artist after he is attacked by the bullies and then takes on karate under his sensei and fights his bullies in a karate competition and proves his worth to other's in the his new home area in China.

Starring

  • Jaden Smith
  • Jackie Chan
  • Taraji P. Henson
