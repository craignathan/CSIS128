Karate Kid
Director Harald Zwart
Lead Actor Jaden Smith
Lead Actress Taraji P. Henson
Rating PG
Karate Kid is a story of a boy from the US who moves to China due to his mother career move. While in his new homeland he is bullied for being american. Later, after the bulling continues Dre Parker (Jaden Smith) takes on Karate from with help from his sensei and takes on his bullies in a karate competition and wins claiming pride from himself in his new home.
